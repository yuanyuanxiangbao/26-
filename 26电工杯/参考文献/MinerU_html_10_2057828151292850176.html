[t in k,t out k]∩[t in k+1,t out k+1]=⌀k=(1,2...n−1) \left[ t _ {\text { in }} ^ {k}, t _ {\text { out }} ^ {k} \right] \cap \left[ t _ {\text { in }} ^ {k + 1}, t _ {\text { out }} ^ {k + 1} \right] = \varnothing \quad k = (1, 2... n - 1)

5.2.3 基于布尔函数的有效遮蔽条件表达

为了便于后文中有效遮挡条件的表述，本文引入了遮蔽状态的布尔函数：

Bool⁡(t)={1, if ∀P2∈Ω,P1P2¯∩C(Pc(t))≠⌀0, otherwise \operatorname{Bool} (t) = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} 1, & \text { if } \forall P _ {2} \in \Omega , \quad \overline {{P _ {1} P _ {2}}} \cap C (P _ {c} (t)) \neq \varnothing \\ 0, & \text { otherwise } \end{array} \right. \tag {5.23}

Bool⁡(tin+)∧¬Bool⁡(tin−)=1 \operatorname{Bool} (t _ {i n} ^ {+}) \land \neg \operatorname{Bool} (t _ {i n} ^ {-}) = 1 \tag {5.24}

T={t∈ℝ+∣Bool(t)=1} T = \{t \in \mathbb {R} ^ {+} \mid B o o l (t) = 1 \} \tag {5.26}
